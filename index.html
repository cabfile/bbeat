About Bytebeat

Bytebeat is a genre of algorithmic music. You write short (or long) formulas (or expressions) and music comes out. It was invented in like 2011 or something idk visit this page

Bytebeat has a range from 0 to 255, Signed has a range from -128 to 127, and Floatbeat from -1 to 1.

Here you can write and listen to expressions. Still a work in progress, though.
